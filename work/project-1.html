Project Overview
Startup that simplifies taxes for individuals and small businesses by providing tools and resources through an easily accessible website and mobile application.
My Contributions
I Designed the website and mobile application. I also Designed the entire ads system including the client on boarding such that its highly effective without compromising the customer experience.
Taxxly
Designer & Developer
Sept 2020 - Present
As I was working with a small trucking company I quickly realized that taxes were a huge headache for small businesses in Botswana as the Burs(The IRS equivalent) website is difficult to navigate and does not have all the information needed in simple terms. The easy solution is to hire tax consultants, however this is expensive for small business especially in the current economic situation.
‍
I designed Taxxly to provide easy to understand guides and tax calculators that make the process easy and fast. For revenue generation I integrated ads into the design in such a way that they are visible without being a pain.

I designed and developed the first version and had some small business owners test it out and give me the feedback. I am now building the second version using the feedback from the previous users and recently acquired knowledge.
